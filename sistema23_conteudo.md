## O QUE É ÚNICO NO SISTEMA 23

O Sistema 23 é o **ponto de partida para quem está começando ou consolidando o básico**. Extraído de 9 episódios do canal "English Evolution Step 1", ele organiza o conteúdo A1-A2 em 5 módulos práticos: os 3 tempos verbais mais usados (passado/presente/futuro), ação com pronomes (eu/você/ele/nós/eles), frases curtas que viram frases completas, preposições e transportes. Tudo com frases de verdade, exemplos de contexto, e progressão clara.

### MÓDULO 2 — VERBOS COM PRONOMES (A1)

**O padrão:** verbo com 6 pronomes — I / We / You / They / He / She  
(He/She adiciona -s no presente simples)

| Verbo | I | We | You | They | He | She |
|-------|---|----|-----|------|----|-----|
| **drive** | I drive to work. | We drive together. | You drive well. | They drive fast. | He drives a taxi. | She drives every day. |
| **ride** | I ride my bike. | We ride horses. | You ride the bus. | They ride scooters. | He rides a motorcycle. | She rides with her friend. |
| **walk** | I walk to school. | We walk together. | You walk fast. | They walk in the park. | He walks home. | She walks every morning. |
| **run** | I run every day. | We run in the park. | You run fast. | They run together. | He runs in the morning. | She runs after work. |
| **eat** | I eat breakfast. | We eat together. | You eat slowly. | They eat at home. | He eats a lot. | She eats salad. |
| **drink** | I drink water. | We drink coffee. | You drink tea. | They drink juice. | He drinks milk. | She drinks water. |
| **work** | I work every day. | We work together. | You work hard. | They work online. | He works at night. | She works from home. |
| **study** | I study English. | We study together. | You study well. | They study a lot. | He studies hard. | She studies every day. |
| **speak** | I speak English. | We speak slowly. | You speak well. | They speak fast. | He speaks clearly. | She speaks softly. |
| **live** | I live here. | We live together. | You live far. | They live nearby. | He lives alone. | She lives with family. |

**Regra -s no He/She/It:**
- verbo normal → + s: work → works, eat → eats, live → lives
- terminado em -sh/-ch/-x → + es: watch → watches
- terminado em consonante + y → ies: study → studies

### MÓDULO 4 — PREPOSIÇÕES NO CONTEXTO (A1)

| Preposição | Significado | Exemplo com contexto |
|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| **at** | em (local específico / hora) | She is **at** school. / The meeting is **at** 9 a.m. |
| **in** | dentro de / em | My phone is **in** my bag. / I live **in** São Paulo. |
| **on** | sobre / em cima de | The book is **on** the table. / I was born **on** Monday. |
| **under** | embaixo de | The cat is **under** the chair. |
| **behind** | atrás de | The boy is **behind** the door. |
| **between** | entre (duas coisas) | The shop is **between** the bank and the café. |
| **next to** | ao lado de | The school is **next to** the park. |
| **in front of** | na frente de | She is **in front of** the building. |
| **near** | perto de | There is a hospital **near** my house. |
| **above** | acima de | The lamp is **above** the table. |

**Phrasal Verbs A1 (com preposição):**

| Phrasal Verb | Significado | Exemplo |
|--------------|-------------|---------|
| **wake up** | acordar | I wake up at 7 every day. |
| **get up** | levantar-se | I get up and have breakfast. |
| **sit down** | sentar | Please sit down. |
| **stand up** | levantar-se (de uma cadeira) | Stand up and come here. |
| **go out** | sair | We go out on Fridays. |
| **come in** | entrar | Please come in! |
| **turn on** | ligar | Turn on the light. |
| **turn off** | desligar | Turn off the TV. |
| **put on** | colocar / vestir | Put on your jacket. |
| **take off** | tirar / decolar | Take off your shoes. |

### MÓDULO 5 — TRANSPORTES EM INGLÊS

| Veículo | Verbo correto | Frase de exemplo |
|---------|---------------|------------------|
| **car** | drive / go by | I go to work by car. / I drive to work. |
| **bus** | take / go by | We wait for the bus every morning. / Take the bus. |
| **taxi** | take | She takes a taxi to the hospital. |
| **train** | take / travel by | They travel to the city by train. |
| **motorbike** | ride | He rides his motorbike to school. |
| **bicycle / bike** | ride | I ride my bicycle in the park. |
| **truck** | drive | The truck carries food to the shop. |
| **plane** | fly / take | I fly to Paris. / We take a plane. |
| **boat** | sail / take | We sail across the lake. |
| **subway / metro** | take | She takes the subway to work. |
| **helicopter** | fly / take | He flies a helicopter. |
| **tram** | take / ride | We take the tram downtown. |
| **ferry** | take / sail | They take a ferry to the island. |
| **scooter** | ride | They ride scooters to school. |
| **horse** | ride | We ride horses on the farm. |

**Verbo correto por veículo:**
- **drive** → carro, caminhão, ônibus (quando é motorista)
- **ride** → bicicleta, moto, scooter, cavalo
- **fly** → avião, helicóptero (piloto)
- **take** → ônibus, trem, metrô, táxi, avião (passageiro)
- **sail** → barco, veleiro
